# Opening Range Breakout

## Overview
Intraday strategy using first 30 minutes to establish range.

## Entry Conditions
- 30-minute range defined
- Price breaks range with volume

## Exit Conditions
- Trailing stop
- End of session

## Risk Parameters
- 2% account risk
- Stop at range boundary

## Setup Patterns
- opening_range_breakout
- 5min_candlestick
