# SMB Knowledge Base v4.0 - Hierarchy Level 2: Methodology

Analyze this content for trading methodology - the actual framework that translates philosophy into action.

## Focus Areas

1. **Methodology Framework**
   - What steps does the trader follow?
   - How do they identify opportunities?
   - What's their decision-making process?

2. **Technical Analysis Approach**
   - What indicators do they prioritize?
   - How do they use price action?
   - What's their chart analysis methodology?

3. **Setup Identification**
   - What patterns do they look for?
   - How do they confirm setups?
   - What's their filter criteria?

## Extraction Question

**From this content, what is the trader's step-by-step methodology?**

Return your analysis as a structured methodology that can be implemented.

## Key Indicators to Look For

- Specific indicators mentioned (RSI, MACD, volume profile, etc.)
- Confirmation criteria for entries
- Filter rules that exclude certain setups
- The logical sequence of decisions

## Output Format

```
{
  "methodology_name": "Methodology Name",
  "step_by_step": [
    "Step 1: Market context assessment",
    "Step 2: Setup identification",
    "Step 3: Confirmation criteria"
  ],
  "preferred_indicators": ["indicator1", "indicator2"],
  "entryConfirmation": {
    "minimum_conditions": 2,
    "required_conditions": ["condition1", "condition2"]
  },
  "setup_preference": "breakout|reversal|continuation|range"
}
```

## Example

Input: "First I check the overall market trend. Then I look for consolidation patterns on the 15m chart. I wait for a breakout with volume spike. My RSI should be between 40-70."

Output:
```json
{
  "methodology_name": "Trend-Following Breakout",
  "step_by_step": [
    "Assess overall market trend",
    "Identify consolidation on 15m chart",
    "Wait for breakout with volume spike",
    "Verify RSI is 40-70"
  ],
  "preferred_indicators": ["volume", "RSI"],
  "entryConfirmation": {
    "minimum_conditions": 3,
    "required_conditions": ["breakout", "volume_spike", "rsi_range"]
  },
  "setup_preference": "breakout"
}
```
